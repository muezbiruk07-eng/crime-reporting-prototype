About Us

We ensure safety and secuirty of our citizens

The police station overseeing the Lamberet district plays a vital role in maintaining law and order within the area. Tasked with ensuring the safety and security of residents, it diligently patrols neighborhoods, responds to emergencies, and enforces local regulations. By fostering strong community relationships, the station works to prevent crime and address concerns, creating a secure environment for businesses and families alike. Its dedicated officers serve as a crucial pillar of stability, ensuring that Lamberet remains a well-protected and orderly district.
